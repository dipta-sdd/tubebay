== Description ==

**TubeBay** is the ultimate bridge between your YouTube channel and your WooCommerce store. Effortlessly enrich your product pages with engaging video content while maintaining peak performance.

Unlike standard video plugins, TubeBay focuses on **Visual Excellence** and **Speed**. Our unique "Video Facade" technology ensures your site stays fast, protecting your SEO and User Experience.

**Key Features:**

*   **Native Gallery Integration**: Replace the main product image or add videos to the gallery strip.
*   **Performance First (Video Facade)**: Only loads YouTube scripts when the user clicks play, keeping your Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) optimized.
*   **Direct Channel Connection**: Sync and browse your YouTube library directly from the WordPress dashboard.
*   **Customizable Player**: Global and per-product controls for Muted Autoplay and Player Controls.
*   **Smooth UX**: Modern, React-powered administration interface for a seamless management experience.
*   **Theme Compatible**: Works with almost any WooCommerce theme by hooking into native gallery filters.

== Installation ==

1.  Upload the `tubebay` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2.  Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  Go to the **TubeBay** menu and connect your YouTube Channel.
4.  Edit any product to assign a video from your library!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does it affect my page speed? =
TubeBay is designed with performance in mind. Our "Video Facade" technique ensures that heavy YouTube iframes only load when requested by the user.

= Can I put the video as the main image? =
Yes! Use the "Replace Main Image" setting to make the video the primary media for your product.
